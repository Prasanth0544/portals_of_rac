## Step-by-Step Posting Guide

### Step 1: Access LinkedIn

1. Go to **www.linkedin.com**
2. Log in with your email and password
3. You'll see your Home Feed

### Step 2: Start Your Post

1. At the top of your feed, find **"Start a post"** box
2. Click anywhere in that box
3. A post creation window will open

### Step 3: Write Your Content

1. Click in the text area
2. Paste your chosen template from above
3. Customize with your details

### Step 4: Add Images (Recommended)

Posts with images get **2x more engagement!**

1. Click the **ğŸ“· Photo icon** at bottom of post window
2. Select your image (dashboard screenshot, architecture diagram)
3. Add up to 4 images

**Suggested images:**
- Screenshot of Admin/TTE/Passenger dashboard
- Architecture diagram
- Stats infographic
- Code snippet

### Step 5: Set Visibility

1. Find dropdown at bottom left (says "Anyone")
2. Select **"Anyone"** for maximum reach
3. This allows recruiters and non-connections to see it

### Step 6: Review & Post

1. Read through your entire post
2. Check for typos
3. Verify links work
4. Click the blue **"Post"** button
